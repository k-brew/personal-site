About the Project

The term "dumpster diving" is often associated with words like disgusting, unsanitary, revolting , malodorous, and other unplesant adjectives and feelings of the sort.


The goal of Diver is change the notion of what "dumpster diving" can mean and why we should replace those negative feelings towards the phrase with words like frugality, charitable, thriftiness, and environmentally-conscience.
